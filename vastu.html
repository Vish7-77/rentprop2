Residential Vastu

  • Vastu for Flats

    Bring good luck and positivity to your flats by following the right Vastu principles. Make sure that they are always on the northeast and eastern side.

  • Vastu for Independent House

    With the freedom to build it from the start, always go for independent houses on the northeast side for good energy and fortune for the space.

  • Vastu for Bungalow

    Vastu planning for the bungalow suggests that all directions are good for its construction, but the shape should be square or rectangular for construction.

  • Vastu for Penthouse

    As per Vastu Shastra, the south and west directions are good directions for construction. Make sure to avoid the east and north direction.

  • Vastu for Farm House

    The farmhouses main building should be in the southwest, west, or south direction of the plot. Cottages can be built all over the land as per Vastu.

    The construction of entrance for apartments can be in the northeast or east direction. According to Vastu, these directions bring positivity and fortune.
